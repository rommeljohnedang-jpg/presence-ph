Who We Are

Built in the Philippines, for Filipino Professionals

Presence PH is a Filipino web design service built for professionals across every field — lawyers, doctors, dentists, architects, home builders, accountants, therapists, consultants, and more.

We noticed that too many skilled professionals in the Philippines either had no website at all, or had one that didn't reflect the quality of their work. Clients and customers are searching online first. If your digital presence doesn't match your real-world credibility, you're leaving trust — and business — on the table.

We're here to fix that. One practice at a time.

A Note on Where We Are We're a small, focused studio — not a large agency with hundreds of past clients. We're honest about that. What we bring is genuine attention to your project, a clear process, and the commitment to deliver something you're proud of. We'd rather build one excellent website than ten forgettable ones.
What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't just principles we post on a wall. They shape every decision we make for our clients.

Honesty First

We don't overpromise. We tell you what your website will and won't do, and we stick to our word on timelines, pricing, and scope.

Quality Over Volume

We take on a limited number of projects so every client gets real attention — not a templated site that looks like everyone else's.

Mobile-First Always

Most of your patients and clients will find you on their phones. Every site we build starts with the mobile experience and works outward.

Speed Matters

Slow websites lose visitors. We hand-code every site with performance in mind — no bloated page builders, no unnecessary scripts.

We Know Your Field

We study the norms of your profession. A dental clinic website should feel different from a legal firm site — and we treat it that way.

Proudly Filipino

We understand the local landscape — what works for Filipino clients, which platforms they use, and what trust looks like in this market.

Our Mission

Why Presence PH Exists

In the Philippines, word-of-mouth still matters — but Google has become the first step before the referral call. A doctor with a clean, fast, professional website builds confidence before the patient even books an appointment. A lawyer with no website makes a prospective client wonder why.

We exist to close that gap. To give Filipino professionals the digital presence that matches the quality of their work — without the complexity of managing it themselves, and without paying a monthly subscription to a platform that doesn't care about their specific practice.

That's what Presence PH is about.
